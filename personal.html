Personal

Badminton

Since early childhood, badminton has been an integral part of my life. What began as a fun way to stay active with friends gradually developed into a true passion. Throughout my youth, I competed at a professional level, with my greatest achievement being the runner-up title at the German Youth Championships in men’s doubles. Today, I continue to compete in Germany’s 2nd Bundesliga with my local club.
